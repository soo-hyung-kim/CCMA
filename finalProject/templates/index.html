About Us

This is the COVID Mutual Aid project. Our aim is to increase students' access to resources that would help reduce the risk of COVID-19 on campus, and to support students who test positive for COVID-19. These resources include but aren't limited to masks, rapid tests and an option to get food delivered to you.
